When I come to Marlowe, I go for the burger.

Last night, I had a HUGE craving for a Marlowe burger. The waitress asked if we wanted to sit at a table or at the counter. I said I needed to sit at the bar because Kraig was serving at the bar. I like Kraig. He is the friendliest bartender.

I ordered my burger medium and with the first bite, I almost fainted. It was that good! It was perfectly prepared. The crispy bacon strips and the flavorful beef patty was super juicy. Fries were just the way I liked it...crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. They were perfect with the spicy horseradish.

Burger goes well with beer. We had Sessions beer.

Custom Ingredient Tags: Beer, Bacon, Horseradish Aioli, No Lettuce.
